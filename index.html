Snowball

Group: Sophia F, Caio, Miguel

This is the dedicated space for the **Snowball** group's project. When the final pages are complete, this section will be replaced with their HTML content.

Mock Content Template:

Snowball represents the idealistic but ultimately exiled intellectual, often compared to Leon Trotsky. His focus on expanding literacy and planning the windmill demonstrated a genuine commitment to improving life on the farm, a vision tragically undermined by Napoleon's opportunism.

Napoleon

Group: Sophia O, Rafaella, Geraldo, Leo

This is the dedicated space for the **Napoleon** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Napoleon embodies the ruthless, totalitarian dictator, mirroring Joseph Stalin. His ascent is marked by manipulation, reliance on the brute force of the dogs, and the gradual erosion of the initial revolutionary principles. He prioritizes personal power over the welfare of the animals.

Clover & Benjamin

Group: Sophia T, Samantha, Joca

This is the dedicated space for the **Clover & Benjamin** group's project.

Mock Content Template:

Clover represents the maternal, questioning part of the working class who suspects the pigs are breaking the Commandments but lacks the intellectual capacity to act. Benjamin, the cynical donkey, represents the intelligentsia who understand the truth but refuse to intervene, believing revolution will always fail.
